드림캐쳐 (Dreamcatcher) are a K-pop/Rock/Electropop group from South Korea.

In 2014, Happyface Entertainment created...ahem..."MINX", and subjected the original five members to performing some of the most egregiously bland and saccharine pop in existence. 'Love Shake' should probably be banned under the Geneva Convention.

Fortunately, in 2016, the people in charge of that particular dark timeline were all fired, and in 2017 two new members were added. They also got a new, rock/metal concept, and they're a lot better.
